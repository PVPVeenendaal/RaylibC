# Fen-like input structure

The input follows this pattern:  W:W18,20,K10:B5,K9,12

. W = White to move
. W18,20,K10 = White pieces on squares 18 and 20, with a king on 10
. B5,K9,12 = Black pieces on squares 5 and 12, with a king on 9
